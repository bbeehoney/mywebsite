About



Bianca Bailey is a young fledling artist, a recent graduate of the Fine Arts program from Brooklyn College, with a varied portfolio. Her work focuses on visualing her and her loved ones with a strong sense of the Old Masters' techniques and aeesthetic. Her works explore the livees of those overlooked in society by displaying snippets of their lives in a painterly and transformative way.
